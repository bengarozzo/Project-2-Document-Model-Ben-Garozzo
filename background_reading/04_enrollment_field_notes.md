# Enrollment Field Notes

## What enrollment represents
Enrollment is typically the number of participants expected or actually enrolled.

## Estimated vs actual
Registry records may label enrollment as "Estimated" or "Actual." Many recent or ongoing trials only report estimated enrollment and may never update it.

## Implication for uncertainty
Analyses that compare enrollment across phases should:
- Report missingness and the share of estimated enrollment
- Use robust summaries (median/IQR) in addition to mean when distributions are skewed
